Artist Statement

I am a Bangladeshi photographer interested in capturing quiet, emotionally resonant moments from everyday life. My work explores mostly themes of isolation, belonging, and the relationship between people and their environments, but sometimes I create images just to showcase the beauty of the subject. I aim to create images that balance documentary realism with personal storytelling.
